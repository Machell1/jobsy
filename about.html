About Jobsy

Jamaica's Premier Service Marketplace

Our Mission

Jobsy is built to solve a simple but important problem: making it easy for Jamaicans to find trusted, skilled service providers in their community. Whether you need a plumber in Mandeville, a hair stylist in Kingston, or an event planner in Montego Bay, Jobsy connects you with the right professional quickly and reliably.

Who We Are

Jobsy is a Jamaican-owned digital platform created to empower local service providers and make life easier for customers across the island. We believe that every skilled worker deserves visibility and every customer deserves quality service.

Our platform features vetted service providers across 10 categories, covering everything from home repair and cleaning to tutoring and events. We are committed to supporting Jamaica's growing gig economy and small business community.

What We Offer

We currently feature service providers across 12 categories serving all 14 parishes, including:

  • Home Services — Plumbing, electrical, carpentry, pest control
  • Cleaning — Residential, commercial, deep cleaning
  • Beauty & Wellness — Hair styling, barbering, nails, massage
  • Automotive — Car repair, detailing, towing
  • Technology — Web development, phone repair, IT support
  • Tutoring & Education — CXC/CAPE prep, music lessons, language classes
  • Events & Entertainment — Planning, catering, DJ, photography
  • Health & Fitness — Personal training, nutrition, wellness
  • Professional Services — Accounting, tax prep, business registration
  • Skilled Trades — Electrical, welding, carpentry
  • Creative Services — Graphic design, photography, branding
  • Construction — Building, renovation, painting, roofing
