About Jobsy

A Jamaican service marketplace

Our Mission

Jobsy makes it easier to find service providers in Jamaica. Need a plumber in Mandeville, a hair stylist in Kingston, or an event planner in Montego Bay? You can search by category and parish to find someone near you.

Who We Are

Jobsy is a Jamaican-owned platform based in Mandeville, Manchester. We built it because finding a reliable service provider in Jamaica usually means asking around or scrolling through social media posts. We wanted something more organized.

The platform lists service providers across 12 categories, from home repair and cleaning to tutoring and events.

What We Offer

We currently feature service providers across 12 categories serving all 14 parishes, including:

  • Home Services: Plumbing, electrical, carpentry, pest control
  • Cleaning: Residential, commercial, deep cleaning
  • Beauty & Wellness: Hair styling, barbering, nails, massage
  • Automotive: Car repair, detailing, towing
  • Technology: Web development, phone repair, IT support
  • Tutoring & Education: CXC/CAPE prep, music lessons, language classes
  • Events & Entertainment: Planning, catering, DJ, photography
  • Health & Fitness: Personal training, nutrition, wellness
  • Professional Services: Accounting, tax prep, business registration
  • Skilled Trades: Electrical, welding, carpentry
  • Creative Services: Graphic design, photography, branding
  • Construction: Building, renovation, painting, roofing

For Service Providers

If you offer a service in Jamaica, you can list it on Jobsy for free. Customers search by category and location, so your listing shows up when people in your area are looking for what you do.

Visit our Contact page to register your business on Jobsy.

Our Values

We check service providers before they go live on the platform. If something goes wrong with a listing or a provider, you can report it and we look into it. We want the platform to be useful, not full of spam.
