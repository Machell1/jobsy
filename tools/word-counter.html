How to Use This Word Counter

Simply type or paste your text into the box above. The word counter updates in real time as you type, showing you the exact number of words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and estimated reading time.

This tool is perfect for writers checking essay word limits, students tracking assignment length, bloggers optimizing content, or anyone who needs a quick character count. All processing happens right in your browser — your text is never sent to any server.

The reading time estimate is based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute, which is the standard for adult readers consuming non-fiction content.
