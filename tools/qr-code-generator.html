How to Use This QR Code Generator

Type any text or paste a URL into the input field, then click "Generate QR Code". Choose your preferred size from the dropdown: small (200px), medium (300px), or large (500px). The QR code is rendered instantly on a canvas element.

Click "Download PNG" to save the QR code as a PNG image file. You can use it in print materials, presentations, business cards, or anywhere you need to share a link or text quickly.

QR codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can be scanned by any smartphone camera. They are widely used for sharing URLs, contact information, Wi-Fi credentials, and more. This tool generates codes entirely in your browser.
