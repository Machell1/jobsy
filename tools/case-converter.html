Free Online Text Case Converter

How to Use This Case Converter

Type or paste your text into the input box, then click any of the conversion buttons. The result appears instantly in the output area. You can convert to UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, Sentence case, camelCase, snake_case, or kebab-case.

Title Case capitalizes the first letter of each word, while Sentence case only capitalizes the first letter after periods and at the start. The programming-style converters (camelCase, snake_case, kebab-case) are perfect for variable naming conventions.

This tool is useful for writers formatting headings, developers converting between naming conventions, and anyone who needs quick text transformation without a code editor.
