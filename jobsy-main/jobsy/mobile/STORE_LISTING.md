## Full Description (Google Play / App Store)

Jobsy is Jamaica's premier service marketplace, connecting hirers with skilled service providers across the island. Whether you need a plumber, a caterer, a photographer, or a contractor, Jobsy makes it easy to post jobs, receive competitive bids, and get quality work done. Discover local events through Pan di Ends, manage contracts, process payments, and grow your business -- all from one powerful app.

### What is Jobsy?

Jobsy is built for the Jamaican community. It brings together people who need services and people who provide them, creating a trusted marketplace where quality work meets fair pricing. From Kingston to Montego Bay, Jobsy helps you find the right professional for any job, or find your next opportunity as a service provider.

### Key Features

Job Posting and Bidding: Post detailed job listings with descriptions, budgets, and timelines. Service providers can browse available jobs and submit competitive bids. Hirers review bids, compare providers, and select the best fit for their needs.

Service Marketplace: Browse a wide range of service categories including home repair, cleaning, landscaping, plumbing, electrical work, catering, photography, event planning, tutoring, and more. Filter by location, rating, price, and availability.

Pan di Ends Events: Discover local events happening across Jamaica. From community gatherings and markets to concerts and festivals, Pan di Ends keeps you connected to what is happening nearby. Advertisers can promote their events to reach a targeted local audience.

Contracts and Agreements: Formalize job arrangements with in-app contracts. Both parties agree to terms before work begins, ensuring clarity on scope, timeline, and payment. Track contract status from start to completion.

Secure Payments: Process payments safely through the app using Stripe integration. Support for credit cards, debit cards, and other payment methods. Payment protection ensures funds are handled securely for both hirers and providers.

Messaging: Communicate directly with service providers or hirers through built-in real-time messaging. Discuss job details, negotiate terms, share images, and coordinate schedules without leaving the app.

Reviews and Ratings: Build your reputation through verified reviews. After each completed job, both hirers and providers can leave ratings and feedback, helping the community identify top-quality professionals.

Role Switching: Seamlessly switch between hirer and provider roles. Post a job in the morning and bid on work in the afternoon. Jobsy adapts to however you want to use it.

Push Notifications: Stay informed with real-time notifications for new bids, messages, contract updates, payment confirmations, and nearby events.

Location-Based Discovery: Find service providers near you using GPS-powered search. View providers on a map and discover services available in your area.

### For Hirers

Post jobs in minutes with detailed descriptions and budget ranges. Receive bids from verified service providers. Compare profiles, reviews, and pricing before making a decision. Manage multiple jobs simultaneously and track progress from start to finish.

### For Service Providers

Browse available jobs that match your skills and location. Submit competitive bids to win new work. Build a professional profile showcasing your expertise, portfolio, and reviews. Grow your client base and manage your business on the go.

### For Advertisers

Promote your events, products, and services to a targeted Jamaican audience. Reach engaged users who are actively looking for local services and events through the Pan di Ends feature.

### Pan di Ends Events

Never miss what is happening in your community. Browse upcoming events by category, date, and location. Get directions, save events, and share them with friends. Event organizers can create listings to maximize attendance and visibility.

Download Jobsy today and join Jamaica's fastest-growing service marketplace. Whether you are hiring, providing services, or looking for local events, Jobsy has you covered.
