Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

FreeToolBox is a collection of free, browser-based tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Processing

All tool processing happens entirely in your web browser. The text, files, passwords, and other data you input into our tools are never transmitted to our servers or any third-party service. Your data stays on your device at all times.

Cookies and Advertising

We may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense on our website. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Analytics

We may use basic website analytics to understand how our tools are used (page views, popular tools, etc.). This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party products and services. These links may be affiliate links, meaning we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on this website are affiliate links. If you click through and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you. This helps us keep the tools free for everyone.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
